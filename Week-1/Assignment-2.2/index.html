Pathanamthitta, Kerala

Pathanamthitta lies in the heart of central Travencore in Kerala. It is the beating heart of the state with a rich culture and heritage. home to Sabarimala. Aranmula boat race, Gavi this gem is a must visit place to truly experience the magic of Kerala

Location

Situated dead center of Kerala Pathanamthitta is a cultural, religious and sporting hotspot.

About

Weather

Tropical climate with high humidity. Dusk and dawns are cool with breeze while day time is hot. Ample sunlight is available throughout the year, monsoons are very strong in the region due to the hilly terrain.

Population

Home to 37,538 residents.

Sports

Pathanamthitta prides itself in the sports and athletics. Volleybal, cricket, football, the world famous snake boat race, are all highlights of this place.

Things To Do

  • Visit the hillstation Gavi.
  • Travel to Aranmula and visit the temple and the banks of river Pamba.
  • Take a short hike up top Chuttipaara in the heart of the Pathanamthitta town.
  • Explore the countless waterfalls, riverbanks, dams and other waterbodies around Pathanamthitta.
  • Indulge yourself in the Vallasadhya held in Aranmula
